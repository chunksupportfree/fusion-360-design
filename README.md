# Fusion 360 Design

## Introduction

Fusion 360 is a cloud-based CAD, CAM, and CAE platform developed by Autodesk. It unifies design, engineering, and manufacturing into a single integrated environment, enabling teams to collaborate on product development from concept through production.

## Features

- Parametric and freeform solid modeling with timeline-based history
- Integrated CAM for CNC machining, turning, and additive manufacturing
- Simulation tools for stress analysis, thermal studies, and modal frequency testing
- Cloud-based collaboration with version control and sharing capabilities
- Generative design powered by AI-driven topology optimization
- Sheet metal design, rendering, and animation tools
